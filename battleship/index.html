Frequently Asked Questions

Come here if you have questions. You can also submit an issue if you find a problem.

How to play?

Click on the grid above to get started. The bottom grid is yours, destroy your opponent's ships before yours gets destroyed!

Who's my opponent?

Your opponent is Yamato, an artificial neural network made of 100 perceptrons. It learns by endlessly fighting itself in a process called machine learning. Dark, isn't it? You can click on Yamato VS Yamato to train it some more.

You didn't make that by yourself, did you?

I used Daniel Shiffman's perceptron. I did the rest myself. You can learn more about it in his playlist Neural Networks.
